## BCBS VT Signature Events

**Blue Sky VT Days℠** is BCBS VT's seasonal outdoor challenge series. Blue Sky VT Days is the umbrella hub/program brand; each seasonal event uses its own consumer-facing name. Four seasons, each paired with a Vermont charity partner:

| Season | Consumer-Facing Event Name | Timing | Charity Partner | 2025 Results | Social Coverage Approach |
|--------|---------------------------|--------|-----------------|-------------|--------------------------|
| Winter | Snow Days | January–March | Vermont Adaptive | 140+ photo submissions; raised $700+ | Snow/outdoor wellness, virtual options; confirmed hashtag: #snowdaysvt |
| Spring | Mountain Days | Spring | Vermont Parks Forever | 85+ in-person participants | Spring outdoors, trail activity |
| Summer | Kayak Days | Summer | Camp Exclamation Point | $1,200+ donated | Outdoor events, community gallery |
| Fall | Apple Days | Fall (Sep–Oct) | Vermont Foodbank | 15,000 lbs apples donated; $1,740 raised | Fall apple picking, orchard events, "Pick a Peck of Fun" |

Note: Use the specific event name (e.g., "Mountain Days 2025," "Snow Days 2026") in social content. "Blue Sky VT Days" is the program hub brand, not the event promotion name.

**Consistent engagement mechanic across all 4 events:** "Tag Blue Cross Vermont in photos on Facebook or Instagram (or email events@bcbsvt.com) and we'll donate $5 to [charity partner] for every photo." Plus a raffle prize. This mechanic is the primary social CTA for every seasonal event.

**Programs and Partnerships** (separate from Blue Sky VT Days):

| Program | Typical Timing | Social Coverage Approach |
|---------|---------------|--------------------------|
| Wellness Revolution (women's cycling) | Summer | Registration promo, event day coverage, recap |
| Girls on the Run Vermont | September | Registration, participant stories, 5K event day |
| Community Advisory Board meetings | Quarterly | Listening/community theme, no identifiable attendees |

## Vermont Seasonal Content Themes

### Winter (Dec–Feb)
- Seasonal affective disorder (SAD) resources
- Indoor wellness: nutrition, sleep, mindfulness
- Cold-weather health tips
- Snow Days event coverage
- New Year benefits activation

### Spring (Mar–May)
- Allergy season health tips
- Getting outside after winter
- Mountain Days launch
- Spring preventive care appointments (annual checkups)
- Better Beginnings® spring cohort

### Summer (Jun–Aug)
- Sun safety and skin care
- Hydration and heat health
- Outdoor event coverage (Kayak Days, Wellness Revolution)
- Kids' summer health and activity
- Telemedicine for summer travel

### Fall (Sep–Nov)
- Flu shot reminders (preventive, covered at $0)
- Open enrollment education surge
- Girls on the Run Vermont coverage
- Gratitude and community impact content
- Year-end benefits checkup

## Content Series Ideas

### "Be Well VT" Blog Amplification
- Every blog post → Instagram quote card + Facebook link post + LinkedIn share
- Carousel format for multi-tip articles

### "Vermonters We Know" Spotlights
- Employee spotlight (with permission): Photo + 3–5 sentence bio + why they love BCBS VT mission
- Member story (anonymized or with signed release): Health journey connected to a program

### "Did You Know?" Series
- Quick facts about BCBS VT programs, plan features, or Vermont health stats
- Works well as Instagram carousels or LinkedIn text posts

### Event Live Coverage
- Pre-event: "Join us!" post 1 week out + day-before reminder
- Day-of: Story coverage (polls, countdown stickers, real-time photos)
- Post-event: Thank-you + photo gallery + recap stat (e.g., "150 hikers joined Mountain Days!")
