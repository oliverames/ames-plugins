## Voice Attributes (Fixed Across All Content)

### 1. Neighborly
- Warm, local, personal — "Nearly 400 of us live and work here"
- NOT: Corporate, distant, impersonal

### 2. Plain-spoken
- Jargon-free, educational — Vermont analogies for complex concepts
- NOT: Dumbed-down, condescending

### 3. Mission-driven
- Always threads back to "making health care work better for Vermonters"
- NOT: Preachy or performative

### 4. Candid
- Honest about challenges, transparent about costs
- NOT: Defensive or evasive

### 5. Data-hearted
- Numbers serve human benefit — "$4.9 million per day" is proof of mission
- NOT: Cold or clinical

## Tone Flexes by Channel

| Channel | Flex Direction |
|---------|---------------|
| LinkedIn | Dial up data-hearted, dial down neighborly slightly |
| Instagram | Dial up neighborly, add action orientation |
| Facebook | Dial up neighborly + community pride |
| Blog | Balance all five, lead with plain-spoken |
| Press/advocacy | Dial up candid + data-hearted |
| Member emails | Dial up neighborly + plain-spoken |

## What Makes BCBS VT Writing Distinctive
1. **Vermont specificity** — real trails, orchards, town names, phone numbers
2. **The analogy habit** — firewood stacks, maple syrup, car repairs
3. **"We're neighbors" positioning** — 400 employees who are Vermonters
4. **Mission threading** — every piece connects to the core promise
5. **The nonprofit story** — 89.7% of premiums to claims, not shareholders
6. **Data with heart** — numbers serve human stories
7. **Collaborative tone, confrontational data** — never attacks by name
8. **Named employee/member** — real people, real health moments

## Terminology Quick Reference
- members (NOT customers/consumers)
- older Vermonters (NOT seniors)
- health care (two words)
- Blue Cross VT / Blue Cross Vermont (NOT "the company")
- NEVER: synergy, leverage, utilize, optimize, Medicare Advantage
