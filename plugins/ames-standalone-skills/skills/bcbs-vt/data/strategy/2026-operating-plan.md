## Four Team Operating Goals (2026)

1. **Financial Health & Affordability** — Educate, inform, and engage Vermonters on our role in helping them access timely and affordable health care.
2. **Attract & Retain High-Value Membership** — Communicate with and market to customers, building retention for our plans and growing membership in targeted areas. (Total 2026 membership context: 191,800.)
3. **Customer Experience Excellence (NPS)** — Strengthen the brand to support retention and local presence strategies.
4. **Run the Business** — Manage and protect the brand through oversight, reviews, and approvals.

## Affordability Matters Campaign (2026's organizing thrust)

The Affordability Matters campaign is the unifying narrative for 2026 external communications. See `data/strategy/affordability-matters.md` for the headline statistic, partner orgs, talking points, and co-branding compliance.

**External tactics:**
- CEO Brand Building (Beth Roberts as thought leader on affordability)
- Continuation of the Affordability Matters campaign
- Speaking events, community letters, video, and blogs
- Refresh the Affordability Matters site (vtaffordablecare.com)
- Brand survey research findings on affordability of health care in VT
- Earned-media: rate-filing support throughout the year, proactive pitches
- Cost Tool promotion and education

**Provider-facing:**
- Surveying providers on referral practices
- Community letters and provider newsletter

**Policymaker-facing:**
- Materials supporting legislator and regulator education on key affordability initiatives

**Internal (employees):**
- Affordability Matters education through multiple channels
- CEO Brand Building communications (Beth's vision)
- Constructing Employee Personas
- Ongoing financial-health education

## How to use this file

- **Drafting any external content for 2026?** Anchor it to one of the four operating goals.
- **Drafting for a specific segment?** Pull the segment row from the audience table — its headline objective is the lens for tone, CTA, and channel choice.
- **Drafting a Medicare-related asset between May and September?** Tie it to the Medicare 101 seminars (Age-In and Switchers).
- **Draft mentions a competitor?** UHC switchers and Cigna→Blue conversion are explicit 2026 plays — competitor framing is allowed when the segment is one of those.
- **Stat claims:** Always source-attribute. The Affordability Matters 19.6% / 7.9% stat traces to WalletHub, July 2025 (citing US Census Bureau and Kaiser Family Foundation).
