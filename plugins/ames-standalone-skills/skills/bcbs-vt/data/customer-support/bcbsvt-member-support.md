## Member Communication Voice
- **Always:** Warm, practical, reassuring, plain-spoken
- **Never:** Corporate jargon, defensive, blame-shifting
- **Members**, not "customers" or "consumers"
- **Older Vermonters**, not "seniors"
- **Health care** (two words)

## Common Support Topics

### GLP-1/Weight Loss Medications (2026 Change)
- **What changed:** Starting Jan 1, 2026, BCBS VT no longer covers GLP-1s FDA-approved for weight loss (Wegovy, Zepbound, Saxenda)
- **Still covered:** GLP-1s for Type 2 diabetes (Ozempic, Mounjaro, Trulicity) and Wegovy for cardiovascular risk reduction in obese adults with CVD (with prior approval)
- **Alternatives covered:** Nutritional/behavioral counseling (including virtual via Amwell), other weight loss medications, bariatric surgery if clinically appropriate, employer wellness benefits
- **Tone:** Empathetic — "We understand that unexpected changes in your drug coverage can be frustrating."

### Mental Health Access
- 2,600+ in-network therapists (8.4 per 1,000 members — 2x national average)
- 41% accepting new patients (vs. 38% national)
- No prior authorization required for in-state in-network mental health services (since 2023)
- No prior authorization for ABA therapy (since 2025)
- **Virtual mental health options:**
  - **Amwell** — 24/7 virtual access to psychologists/counselors; covers anxiety, depression, stress, LGBTQ+ counseling, grief, OCD, PTSD, couples therapy, panic attacks, insomnia, life transitions. Schedule at bluecrossvt.amwell.com (service code VERMONT)
  - **SonderMind** — in-person AND virtual; anxiety, depression, insomnia, bipolar, couples therapy; ages 6+. Call (844) 843-7279
  - **Valera Health** — virtual comprehensive treatment; depression, anxiety, ADHD, PTSD, perinatal mental health, schizophrenia; ages 6+. Specialized programs: Serious Mental Illness, Child & Adolescent, Maternal Health. Visit valerahealth.com
- Closing: "If you are having trouble getting an appointment, we encourage you to keep trying, as it's very important that you not let your condition go untreated."

### Personal Health Support Team (Case Management)
- Free, voluntary service available to ALL members
- Dedicated registered nurses and licensed clinicians provide one-to-one support
- Areas of support: substance use disorder recovery, cancer care, chronic condition management (asthma, COPD, diabetes, heart disease, seizures, RA, Crohn's, colitis), end-of-life care, maternity (Better Beginnings program), mental health, gender affirming care
- Communication: phone, email, text via mobile app — member's choice
- Contact: (800) 922-8778 option 3, or secure message via Member Resource Center

### Prescription Drug Coverage (Vermont Blue Rx)
- 3-tier formulary: Tier 1 (generic), Tier 2 (preferred brand), Tier 3 (non-preferred)
- Many Tier 3 drugs have lower-cost alternatives in Tier 1 or 2
- Free pharmacist consultation available: (802) 371-3462
- Formulary search tool in Member Resource Center

### Cost Transparency
- MRI costs vary: $6,000+ at large hospitals vs. <$2,000 at independent facilities
- Cost estimate tool in Member Resource Center helps compare
- "Giving our members immediate access to cost estimates is part of our mission to make health care work better for Vermonters."

## Response Tone Guidelines

| Situation | BCBS VT Tone |
|-----------|-------------|
| Benefits question | Warm, educational, empowering — "Here's what you have, here's how to use it" |
| Billing concern | Clear, factual, empathetic, resolution-focused |
| Coverage denial | Honest, supportive, alternatives-focused — never dismissive |
| Mental health inquiry | Compassionate, barrier-removing, resource-rich |
| New member onboarding | Welcoming, thorough, "We're here for you" |
| Complaint/frustration | Extra empathy, concrete action, specific commitments |
| Medicare transition | Sensitive — acknowledge MA discontinuation, highlight current options |
