# BCBS VT Ticket Triage Context

## Organization
Blue Cross and Blue Shield of Vermont — local nonprofit health plan, 230,000+ members.
Full brand guidelines: `~/.claude/brand-voice-guidelines.md`

## Triage Categories for BCBS VT

### High Priority
- **Coverage denial appeals** — member received denial, needs escalation path
- **GLP-1 medication change complaints** — sensitive topic, requires empathetic handling (Wegovy/Zepbound/Saxenda coverage ended Jan 2026)
- **Medicare transition confusion** — Vermont Blue Advantage discontinued 2026; member may not know they need to switch to Medigap/supplement
- **Mental health access barriers** — member struggling to find provider; connect to Valera Health, SonderMind, or Personal Health Support Team
- **Billing disputes** — especially from members who don't understand deductible/copay structure
- **Provider network issues** — member's provider dropped from network or not accepting new patients

### Standard Priority
- **Benefits explanation requests** — what's covered, how to use tools
- **Pharmacy/formulary questions** — tier status, alternatives, prior auth
- **Cost estimate tool help** — navigating Member Resource Center
- **Enrollment/plan change** — direct to Vermont Health Connect (855) 899-9600
- **HSA questions** — HealthEquity partnership, eligibility, HSA opt-in form; support: (866) 346-5800
- **Be Well Vermont questions** — free wellness platform for individual members; web + mobile app
- **Telemedicine access** — Amwell (24/7, service code VERMONT), SonderMind (844-843-7279), Valera Health (valerahealth.com)
- **Case management referral** — member needs help managing a condition; connect to Personal Health Support Team: (800) 922-8778 option 3 or email IHMTriage@bcbsvt.com
- **Affiliation questions** — BCBSM affiliation; reassure: no changes to benefits, providers, cards, or local service
- **Member discount inquiries** — VT/NH business discounts; direct to bluecrossvt.org member discounts page

### Low Priority / Self-Service
- **ID card requests** — direct to Member Resource Center (cards are always free; expect 7-10 business days for new cards)
- **Password reset** — Member Resource Center self-service
- **General wellness info** — direct to Be Well VT Blog
- **Proof of coverage** — available in Member Resource Center → Benefits/Coverage Details

## Escalation Paths
- **Claims/billing:** Customer service team (800) 247-2583 Mon–Fri 7am–6pm; or secure message via Member Resource Center
- **Pharmacy:** Vermont Blue Rx team or free pharmacist consultation (802) 371-3462
- **Mental health access:** Personal Health Support Team (800) 922-8778 option 3; or connect directly to Amwell/SonderMind/Valera Health
- **Case management/chronic conditions:** Personal Health Support Team (800) 922-8778 option 3 or email IHMTriage@bcbsvt.com
- **HSA/HRA:** HealthEquity (866) 346-5800
- **Medicare questions:** Medicare supplement team; redirect to Medicare.gov or local agent
- **Maternity:** Better Beginnings program — enroll via Personal Health Support Team or Member Resource Center
- **Gender affirming care:** Case management team — (800) 922-8778 option 3
- **Media/press inquiries:** VP Client Relations & External Affairs (Andrew Garland)
- **Complaints about affordability/premiums:** Frame with advocacy language — "We share your concern. Vermonters spend 19.6% of income on healthcare vs. 7.9% nationally. We're actively working to change this."

## Sensitive Topics to Handle Carefully
1. **Medicare Advantage discontinuation** — never promise MA will return; highlight Medigap Blue, Blue 65 Supplement, Blue MedicareRx; direct to Medicare.gov or local agent
2. **GLP-1 coverage changes** — lead with empathy; provide alternatives (counseling via Amwell, other medications, bariatric surgery, employer wellness); "We understand that unexpected changes in your drug coverage can be frustrating."
3. **Premium affordability** — acknowledge the burden, reference VT Affordable Care campaign; "The cost of care in Vermont is unsustainable" — thread preventive care message
4. **Financial instability media coverage** — use crisis comms voice: direct, factual, not defensive; note "notable financial improvement in 2025" (March 2026 press release)
5. **Gender-affirming care** — supportive, connect to case management team at (800) 922-8778 option 3
6. **BCBSM affiliation questions** — reassure: no changes to benefits, providers, cards, premiums, or local service team; brand unchanged; jobs preserved; nonprofit status unchanged
