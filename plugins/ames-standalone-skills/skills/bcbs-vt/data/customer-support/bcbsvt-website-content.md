# BCBS VT Website Content Reference

Source content from bluecrossvt.org for use in KB article creation. All data scraped from the live site.

## Product Details (2026)

### Individual & Family Metal Tiers
Each plan page includes: monthly premium, deductible, OOP max, copays, coinsurance, pharmacy benefits.
- **Platinum** — lowest deductible/OOP, highest premium
- **Vermont Preferred Gold** — 4-8-12 zero-dollar visits (primary care, mental health, substance use)
- **Silver CDHP Reflective** — mid-range, with reflective pricing
- **Bronze CDHP** — highest deductible, lowest premium, HSA-eligible

### Employer Plans (1–100 employees)
- Same metal tiers as individual
- **Blue Edge Plans** — additional employer-focused options
- **HRA/FSA/HSA options** — through HealthEquity partnership
- Employer resources: enrollment guides, premium charts, wellness programs

### Vision Coverage
Available as add-on to health plans.

### HSA Details
- Through HealthEquity partnership
- Tax-advantaged: tax-deductible contributions, tax-free earnings, tax-free qualified distributions
- Money rolls over annually; portable between plans/employers
- 2026 expansion: more Individual/Family QHPs now HSA-eligible (federal legislation)
- HealthEquity features: online portal (24/7), Visa debit card, dedicated support at (866) 346-5800
- Opt-in: bluecrossvt.org/hsa-optin

## Member Tools & Self-Service

### Member Resource Center Features
1. **My Profile** — plan status, PCP, order ID cards, proof of coverage
2. **Benefits/Coverage Details** — outline of coverage, OOP costs, service limitations
3. **Claims & Summary** — 18 months history, search by date, deductible/OOP tracking; pharmacy claims via Vermont Blue Rx portal
4. **HSA/HRA** — HealthEquity account management
5. **Forms** — electronic submission (other insurance, Better Beginnings enrollment)
6. **Authorizations** — prior approval services list, status tracking, confirmation letters
7. **Secure Messaging** — 1 business day response; route to specific departments; urgent marking available

### Cost Estimate Tool
- Compare provider costs for medical services
- Enhanced version launched Dec 2025
- MRI example: $6,000+ at large hospitals vs. <$2,000 at independent facilities

### Text Health Alerts
Members can sign up for text reminders about preventive care and health management.

## Wellness & Prevention Programs

### Be Well Vermont
- Free for individual plan members
- Covers: nutrition, chiropractic care, stress management, yoga, meditation, exercise
- Web platform + mobile app
- Based on "tiny habits" approach (BJ Fogg research)
- YouTube tutorial available

### Be Well at Work
- Worksite wellness for eligible businesses
- Helps employers improve employee health and wellbeing

### Member Discounts
- Participating VT/NH businesses
- Categories: acupuncture, fitness, yoga, martial arts, massage, personal coaching, spa, equipment, hearing aids, mental health/wellness, TCM
- Filterable by Vermont county + "Virtual" category
- Members can suggest new businesses

### Preventive Care Focus (CMO Tom Weigel messaging)
- "We must flip the script from reactive care to proactive care"
- Free preventive screenings
- Home drug infusion therapy (lower hospital exposure + costs)
- 625,000+ telehealth visits in 2024

## Mental Health & Telehealth

### Network Stats
- 2,600+ in-network therapists (8.4 per 1,000 members — 2x national average)
- 41% accepting new patients
- No prior auth for in-state in-network mental health services (since 2023)
- No prior auth for ABA therapy (since 2025)

### Amwell (24/7 Virtual)
- Psychologists + counselors
- bluecrossvt.amwell.com (service code VERMONT)
- Conditions: anxiety, depression, stress management, LGBTQ+ counseling, bereavement/grief, OCD, PTSD/trauma, couples therapy, panic attacks, insomnia, life transitions
- Available throughout the U.S.

### SonderMind (In-Person + Virtual)
- Anxiety, depression, insomnia, bipolar disorder, couples therapy
- Ages 6+ (under 6: contact directly)
- (844) 843-7279

### Valera Health (Virtual Comprehensive)
- Depression, anxiety, ADHD, PTSD, perinatal mental health, OCD, schizophrenia, follow-up after hospitalization
- Ages 6+
- Specialized programs: SMI, Child & Adolescent, Maternal Health
- valerahealth.com
- Partnership announced Oct 2023

## Case Management (Personal Health Support Team)

### Service Details
- Free, voluntary, confidential
- One-to-one dedicated clinician (registered nurse or licensed clinician)
- Phone, email, text via app — member chooses
- Business hours 8am–4:30pm; after-hours available
- Proactive outreach (e.g., post-hospitalization)

### Contact
- (800) 922-8778, option 3
- Email: IHMTriage@bcbsvt.com
- Secure message via Member Resource Center → Case Management

### Areas
Substance use recovery, cancer care, chronic conditions (asthma, COPD, diabetes, heart disease, seizures, arthritis, Crohn's, colitis), end-of-life, maternity (Better Beginnings), mental health, gender affirming care

## BCBSM Affiliation

### Key Facts
- Blue Cross VT is now a subsidiary of BCBS Michigan
- NOT a merger — preserves local governance, VT workforce, nonprofit status, brand
- HQ remains Berlin, VT
- No changes to: benefits, provider network, cards, premiums, local customer service
- Award-winning customer service team unchanged (2024 Call Center of the Year)

### Why
- Combined scale against for-profit competitors
- Shared technology and innovation
- Better products for Vermont market
- History of collaboration (shared claims platform, Vermont Blue Advantage launch 2021)
