## Key Knowledge Areas

### Plans & Enrollment
- Individual/family plans through Vermont Health Connect
- Three plan types: Vermont Preferred (4-8-12 zero-dollar visits), Vermont Select (HSA-integrated), Standard
- Metal tiers: Platinum, Gold, Silver, Silver CDHP Reflective, Bronze
- Open enrollment period: typically Nov–Jan (check VT Health Connect for current dates)
- How to enroll: visit Vermont Health Connect at info.healthconnect.vermont.gov or call (855) 899-9600
- HSA: Health Savings Account paired with Vermont Select plans; tax-advantaged for medical expenses; calculator at secure.bcbsvt.com/hsaCalculator.php
- Employer plans: Blue Edge plans for small groups (1–100); HRA, FSA, HSA options available
- Vision: Vision coverage available as add-on
- Medicare: Medigap Blue, Blue 65 Supplement, Blue MedicareRx (Part D)
- ⚠️ Medicare Advantage (Vermont Blue Advantage) discontinued Jan 1, 2026 — "The Medicare Advantage market in Vermont has been severely affected by payers choosing to leave the Medicare Advantage market and high utilization of medical care." Redirect members to Medicare.gov or a local agent for supplement options

### Pharmacy (Vermont Blue Rx)
- 3-tier formulary system
- Free pharmacist consultation: (802) 371-3462
- Formulary search in Member Resource Center
- GLP-1 coverage change (Jan 2026): weight loss GLP-1s no longer covered; diabetes/cardiovascular GLP-1s still covered

### Wellness Programs
- Be Well Vermont platform — free for individual members; nutrition, chiropractic, stress management, yoga, meditation, exercise; web + mobile app
- Personal Health Support Team — free one-to-one case management by registered nurses and licensed clinicians: substance use, cancer, chronic conditions (asthma, COPD, diabetes, heart disease, seizures, RA, Crohn's, colitis), end-of-life, maternity, mental health, gender affirming care. Contact: (800) 922-8778 option 3
- Better Beginnings for expectant mothers
- Lifestyle and Medication Guidance Program
- Be Well at Work — worksite wellness program for eligible businesses
- Member Discounts — discounts at VT/NH businesses promoting healthy lifestyles (fitness, yoga, acupuncture, coaching, massage). Browse by county/category at bluecrossvt.org
- Gold Bell Seal for Workplace Mental Health (2025)

### Mental Health
- 2,600+ in-network therapists
- No prior auth for in-state in-network services (since 2023)
- No prior auth for ABA therapy (since 2025)
- 625,000+ telehealth visits in 2024
- **Amwell** — 24/7 virtual psychologists/counselors; anxiety, depression, stress, LGBTQ+ counseling, grief, OCD, PTSD, couples therapy, panic attacks, insomnia, life transitions. bluecrossvt.amwell.com (service code VERMONT)
- **SonderMind** — in-person + virtual; anxiety, depression, insomnia, bipolar, couples therapy; ages 6+. Call (844) 843-7279
- **Valera Health** — virtual comprehensive treatment; depression, anxiety, ADHD, PTSD, perinatal mental health, schizophrenia, follow-up after hospitalization; ages 6+. Specialized programs: Serious Mental Illness (SMI), Child & Adolescent, Maternal Health. valerahealth.com

### Cost Transparency & Affordability
- VT households spend 19.6% on healthcare (vs. 7.9% national)
- VT Affordable Care campaign: vtaffordablecare.com
- Cost estimate tool helps members compare provider prices
- MRI example: $6,000+ at large hospitals vs. <$2,000 at independent facilities
- "Choosing a lower-cost provider can still save you money in the future, by reducing the need for additional premium increases."
