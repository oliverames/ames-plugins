# BCBS VT Messaging Pillars

## Pillar 1: Vermont's Local Nonprofit

**Core claim:** Blue Cross VT is the only local, not-for-profit health plan in Vermont — and that makes all the difference.

**Key messages:**
- 40+ years of serving Vermonters
- No shareholders — dollars go to member claims and community
- Employees are Vermonters who know the state
- Local decisions, not national mandates
- In 2025: paid $1.785 billion in member claims (~$4.9 million per day) [UPDATE ANNUALLY — source: BCBS VT annual report]
- Nearly 90% of every premium dollar received goes to paying member claims (featured on Facebook; use for nonprofit differentiation)

**Do/Don't:**
- ✅ "Our nonprofit structure isn't just a legal designation — it's a commitment to make health care work better."
- ✅ "We're Vermonters ourselves."
- ❌ "As an insurance carrier operating in the state of Vermont..."

## Pillar 2: Community First

**Core claim:** Blue Cross VT is a community partner, not just a policy provider.

**Key messages:**
- Blue Sky VT Days℠ (seasonal outdoor challenges — Winter/Spring/Summer/Fall; formerly Mountain Days + Kayak Days)
- Wellness Revolution (women's cycling program, in partnership with Terry Bicycles)
- Girls on the Run Vermont (statewide sponsor; grades 3–5 Girls on the Run + grades 6–8 Heart & Sole)
- Vermont Caring for Children Foundation
- Community Advisory Boards in Windham County, Northeast Kingdom, Rutland County
- Physical activity grants for schools and workplaces
- Walking maps, regional events
- Charitable giving enables local organizations to expand

**Do/Don't:**
- ✅ "We host events throughout the year to help Vermonters get outside each season."
- ✅ "We're constantly listening to community members to get their feedback and ideas."
- ❌ "Our community outreach initiatives align with CSR objectives."

## Pillar 3: Health & Well-being

**Core claim:** We help Vermonters live healthier lives — not just pay their medical bills.

**Key messages:**
- Free wellness programs (Be Well Vermont portal)
- Better Beginnings® for expectant mothers
- Personal Health Support Team for chronic conditions
- Lifestyle and Medication Guidance Program (pharmacist consultations)
- Telemedicine via Amwell® (24/7; service key: VERMONT at bluecrossvt.amwell.com)
- Mental health telehealth via Valera Health and SonderMind®
- Gold Bell Seal for Workplace Mental Health (2025)

**Do/Don't:**
- ✅ "Helping you and your family live happier, healthier lives is our goal."
- ✅ "As your health care partner, we provide trusted advice to help you improve your health and wellness."
- ❌ "Utilize our wellness platform to optimize your health outcomes."

## Pillar 4: Transparency & Trust

**Core claim:** We make health care easier to understand and navigate.

**Key messages:**
- Cost estimate tool — shop and compare before care
- Formulary search — find lower-cost drug alternatives
- Plain-language coverage explanations
- Award-winning Vermont-based customer service (2024 Call Center of the Year) [UPDATE ANNUALLY]
- Members can trust Blue Cross VT to never ask for personal info by phone

**Do/Don't:**
- ✅ "You can get estimates for car repairs. Why should health care be any different?"
- ✅ "Giving our members immediate access to cost estimates is part of our mission to make health care work better for Vermonters."
- ❌ "Our enhanced cost transparency module provides price comparison functionality."

---

## Pillar 5: Vermont Healthcare Advocacy

**Core claim:** Blue Cross VT is working to fix Vermont's healthcare system for everyone.

**Key messages:**
- Vermont healthcare costs are driven by high hospital prices and increased utilization
- Vermont households spend 19.6% of income on healthcare vs. 7.9% national average (#1 most expensive, WalletHub 2025)
- Blue Cross VT advocates for system-wide reform, not just rate increases
- CEO Beth-Ann Roberts: "all stakeholders come to the table to listen and collaborate"
- Collaborating with legislators, regulators, providers, and other stakeholders
- Transparency: issued public correction to "brink of bankruptcy" media reports
- **VT Affordable Care campaign** (Dec 2025–present): Coalition with 4 independent facilities challenging hospital pricing; campaign site vtaffordablecare.com with procedure cost comparisons. Tagline: "Each of us can help lower the cost of our healthcare without sacrificing quality." Consumer empowerment frame — "you have more affordable, quality options."

**Do/Don't:**
- ✅ "The health care problems in Vermont are significant, and it will require all stakeholders to come to the table."
- ✅ "We're working closely with regulators, legislators, government and health care partners."
- ✅ "Vermonters Pay the Most in America for Healthcare" (VT Affordable Care campaign headline)
- ❌ (Never attack competitors or blame specific parties by name in BCBS VT's own voice — the data does the work)
- ❌ "Vermont Blue Advantage" or any Medicare Advantage product reference (discontinued 2026)

## Audience-Specific Message Maps

### Individual Members
Priority pillars: 3 (Health), 4 (Transparency), 1 (Local Nonprofit)
Key differentiator: "We know you, and we're here for you when it matters most."

### Employers/Groups
Priority pillars: 1 (Local Nonprofit), 4 (Transparency), 2 (Community)
Key differentiator: "Local expertise, flexible plans, and 150 years of combined account management experience."

### Medicare/Seniors
Priority pillars: 3 (Health), 4 (Transparency), 1 (Local Nonprofit)
Key differentiator: "A big part of healthy aging is having the right plan in place — we're here to help you find it."
⚠️ **IMPORTANT (as of 2026):** Vermont Blue Advantage discontinued all Medicare Advantage plans in 2026. Do NOT reference or imply Medicare Advantage availability. Current Medicare offerings are: Vermont Medigap Blue, Vermont Blue 65 Supplement, and Blue MedicareRx (Part D).

### Media/Press
Priority pillars: 5 (Advocacy), 1 (Local Nonprofit), 2 (Community)
Key differentiator: Vermont's only local nonprofit voice on healthcare policy.

### Internal/Employees
Priority pillars: 2 (Community), 3 (Health), 1 (Local Nonprofit)
Key differentiator: "94% of employees say this is a great place to work — because our mission matters."
