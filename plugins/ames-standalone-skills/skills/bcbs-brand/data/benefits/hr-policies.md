# BCBS VT HR Policies & Culture Reference

## Remote Work Policy (2026)

**Source document:** `~/Documents/BCBS/Administrative/2026 BlueCross BlueShield of Vermont Remote Work Policy.pdf`

Blue Cross VT offers a remote work option for eligible roles. Oliver Ames's Social Media Strategist role is approved for remote work per the 2026 policy. Read the full PDF for complete terms.

**Key points (from onboarding context):**

- "Think creatively, make decisions, and take action" — employees are trusted to manage their time

## Onboarding Expectations

**First 3 months:**
- Maximum 3 days PTO during ramp-up period (per offer call)
- Focus on getting "feet underneath you" — learning systems, team, and workflows
- Connect with key contacts: Beth (HR), Ashley (Marketing), team members

**Start date:** March 2026 (exact date TBD with Beth)

**Key onboarding steps:**
1. Review New Hire Benefits Summary PDF to enroll in healthcare/401k
2. Set up Be Well Vermont wellness program account (to start earning $500/year)
3. Activate HSA — contributions begin January/July cycles
4. Confirm 401k contribution level (recommend 6% to maximize company match)
5. Review Remote Work Policy

## Culture & Values

**Workplace identity:**
- Mission-driven: "Make health care work better for all Vermonters"
- 94% of employees say it's a great place to work (internal survey)
- Small, self-organizing teams; agile mindset
- Employees empowered to "think creatively, make decisions, and take action"
- Strong community commitment — employees participate in events (Mountain Days, Wellness Revolution, Kayak Days)

**Mental health:** 2025 Gold Bell Seal for Workplace Mental Health (Mental Health America). Genuine commitment — this is the company's own product area.

**Diversity & inclusion:** Blue Cross VT emphasizes inclusive healthcare; D&I framing is integrated into product and community work, not siloed.
