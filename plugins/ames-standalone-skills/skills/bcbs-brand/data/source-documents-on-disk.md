category. Read these for additional context as needed — do not move or
modify them. The authoritative-* mirrors in `data/brand/` and the strategy
mirrors in `data/strategy/` are sourced from the `Reference/` subdirectory
below; if a mirror disagrees with its source PDF, treat the source PDF as
authoritative and update the mirror.
